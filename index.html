Welcome to
Beach Luxury Hotel, Resort & Spa Club,
Beach & Pool, Restaurant & Bar,
Dublin Ireland!
The four-star Beach Hotel, is a family run independent hotel. We first opened our door's in 1930 and have continued to grow ever since. Having the pleasure of hosting many events including Weddings, Christenings & Family gatherings and Corporate Events throughout the years. you're sure to enjoy your visit to the Beach Hotel.
A warm welcome awaits you on arrival to the hotel. Offering ideal leisure breaks, The extensive recently refurbished 2020, The Beach Hotel Bar and The Beach Hotel Restaurant offers excellent value in delicious wholesome food, sourced fresh from local suppliers where possible.
Enjoy some of the local attractions in this beautiful undulating countryside and surrounding areas. Get to know your family tree while on your break... Beach Hotel is the best place you can visit today and tomorrow. We are waiting for you!
